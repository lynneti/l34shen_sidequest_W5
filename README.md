## Description

This sketch creates a reflective, meditative camera experience within a world larger than the screen. The camera smoothly follows the player as they move through space, using easing, pacing, and subtle motion to evoke a calm emotional tone. Hidden symbols are scattered throughout the world for the camera to “discover,” encouraging slow exploration rather than fast gameplay.

---

## Learning Goals

Implement a camera system that follows a player within bounded world coordinates
Use camera clamping to prevent viewing outside the world
Apply easing (lerp) to create smooth, emotionally expressive motion
